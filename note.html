Adaptive Fraud and Scam Detection

System Overview: This system, Adaptive Fraud and Scam Detection Using Gemini LLM with OCR and Verified Sender Analysis, is designed to help users detect fraudulent messages, suspicious URLs, and scam-related content. It combines AI text analysis, image text extraction, and verification checks to provide an accurate risk assessment.

The system adapts to different forms of scams—emails, SMS, social media messages, screenshots, PDFs, and website links—making it useful for everyday users, students, and professionals who want to ensure digital safety.

How the System Works

The system uses three major components:

  • Gemini LLM Text Analysis – Detects scam indicators such as phishing keywords, threatening language, fake promotions, impersonation, and unusual requests.
  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition) – Extracts text from images such as screenshots, photos, or scanned documents.
  • Verified Sender Analysis – Evaluates if the sender, URL, or content is associated with known scam markers or unsafe characteristics.

The system then calculates a risk percentage and classifies the input into:

  • Low Risk – Safe or harmless content.
  • Medium Risk – Suspicious traits found; caution recommended.
  • High Risk – Strong evidence of scam or fraud attempt.

How to Use the System

  1. Choose whether you want to analyze Text/File or a URL.
  2. Paste the message OR upload a file (text, PDF, or image) OR enter a website link.
  3. Click “Analyze”. It will only run when the required input is provided.
  4. View results including classification, risk percentage, explanation, and safety recommendations.

How It Works

The Adaptive Fraud and Scam Detector combines advanced AI, OCR technology, and verified sender analysis to identify scams across multiple platforms. Here's a step-by-step overview:

  • Step 1: Input Submission – Users can submit suspicious text, upload a file (PDF, image, or text), or paste a URL for analysis.
  • Step 2: Text & File Analysis – The system uses the Gemini LLM to detect phishing, scam language, and impersonation patterns in text. OCR extracts text from images and scanned documents.
  • Step 3: URL & Sender Verification – URLs and sender information are cross-checked against known malicious sources and scam markers.
  • Step 4: Risk Assessment – The system calculates a risk score and classifies content into Low, Medium, or High Risk categories.
  • Step 5: Recommendations – Users receive detailed guidance on how to handle suspicious messages, links, or files safely.

This multi-layered approach ensures a comprehensive and adaptive defense against evolving scam techniques.
